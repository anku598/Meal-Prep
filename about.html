Welcome to Meal Prep

Meal Prep Dhaka is Bangladesh's first and only nutritionally balanced, perfectly portioned meal delivery service. We provide a wide variety of freshly cooked calorie-counted meals to individuals across Dhaka. Our team of creative chefs and dieticians work together to ensure that your meal is not just healthy, but also tasty and varied. The meals are individually packed in hygienic, contamination-proof packages with a clear account of the nutritional content printed on each package. We also provide healthy catering options to offices. Our meals are not about bland, dull, diets, self deprivation, or complicated fad diets. They are a lifestyle choice - providing balanced nutritious meals designed for your body's needs. Each meal is designed to provide balanced nutrition - a judicious mix of carbohydrates and proteins, a high fibre content, low fat content and minimal cholesterol content.

Why Meal Prep

Goals & Vision

Meal prepping helps you stay on track with your daily food/calorie/ macro consumption. There is a great quote that states: “fail to plan, plan to fail”. This couldn’t be any more true. When you meal prep all of your daily meals and snacks are already planned out, therefore, there is no wiggle room to eat an unhealthy snack or eat an extra cheat meal. I know that if I make my meals ahead of time I feel obligated to eat them and not waste food which keeps me from eating unnecessary and unhealthy foods.

You WILL save money if you decide to start meal prepping. Going to the supermarket and buying one pound of ground beef and some vegetable to create a stir fry for 2-3 meals is BY FAR cheaper than going to BFC 3 days in a row for lunch. Eating out at a restaurant is expensive, so why not save yourself some money and cook your own meals ahead of time

Not only will you save money, but, you’ll save yourself some time. Imagine getting home from the gym and popping your meal into the microwave for 2 minutes and then BOOM, it’s done, time to eat. Now you have so much more free time to do something BESIDES cooking, planning or grocery shopping. It can also save you time in the morning if you prepare your breakfast ahead of time. More time in the morning for me usually means more sleep and ultimately meaning I am a happier human throughout the day. More time AND money? Hallelujah!!

To reach your health and fitness goals is also a good reason to meal prep. By weighing your food, counting calories and planning meals ahead of time, you’re able to stay on track and meet your ultimate goal. Meal prepping once a week where you divide up your meals and snacks helps you maintain a firm grip on your final goal because you are allowing yourself the tools to get there. If you’re flying by the seat of your pants and grabbing whatever is most convenient at that time is not the way to follow a weight-loss or fitness plan and makes it easier to cheat and lose sight of your goals.
